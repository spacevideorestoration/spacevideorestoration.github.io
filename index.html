	in relation to inner and outer space. Having noticed that there are
	shared aesthetic qualities of video imagery that accompany disparate
	cultural and scientific phenomena including guided meditation,
	hypnosis, undersea and space exploration by NASA, motivational
	speaking, powerpoint backgrounds, science fiction, psychedelic
	drug culture, electronic music, popular spirituality, and computer
	effects, we have built a generative system that mixes an original
	non-linear narrative with YouTube videos on these subjects as they
	are uploaded in real time. These videos often attempt to portray
	what are ultimately non-visual spaces, producing images that are at
	once placeholders, images of the transcendental, trippy intergalactic
	stereotypes, and fields for persuasion.<br><br></div>
